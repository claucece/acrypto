ASCrypto2023 will be co-located with LatinCrypt2023 on the 2nd and 3rd of October, 2023 in Quito, Ecuador.

It is an official IACR Cryptology School.


Chairs:

  • Sofía Celi, Cryptography Researcher, Brave
  • Diego Aranha Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Aarhus University, Denmark

Venue:

Universidad de las Fuerzas Armadas, ESPE, Av. Gral. Rumiñahui S/N, Sangolquí 171103, Ecuador.


Registration Cost:

20USD (plus Ecuadorian taxes).
